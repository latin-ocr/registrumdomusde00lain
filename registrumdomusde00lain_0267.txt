Litera pensionis Alexandri Makneill notarii publici.

To all and findry . . . maister Johnne Dyngwaile prothonotar apostolic
provest of the Trinite College befid Edinburgh and the prebendaris of the
samyne greting . . . For the gude trew and thankfull seruice to ws done
and to be done be our louit Alexander Makneill notar public in the hal-
din of our heid courtis and making of prooeffis and euidentis . . . in tyme
to eum concerning . . . our fiaid college - wit yhe ws herefor cheptourlie
gadderit . . . to haue gewyne and grantit . . . to the said Alexander indu-
ring all tho dayis of his lyf ane yheirlie pencioun of fourty schillingis . . .
mony of Seotland . . . begynnand the first payment thairof at the feist of
Witsouneday nixt to eum . . . He haldand our said heid courtis and doand ws
thankfull seruice thairfor as said is . . . And gif we caus the said Alexander
to ryd or pas [on] ony eraudis of ouris concerning the weill of our said
college furth of Edinburch . . . we sall beyr and mak 1115 expenfis sufficientlie
as efferis in sa lang as he romanis thairintill . . . At our said college the
penult day of Marehe the yheir of God ane thousand fyf hundreith and

tuenty fyf yheiris.
